English Muffin Pizzas

They just don't do it like the english anymore

Ingredients

  • 4 English muffins, split
  • 1/2 cup canned pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 16 slices pepperoni sausage

Steps

  1. 1: Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. 2: Arrange the English muffin halves cut-side up on a baking sheet.
  3. 3: Spread pizza sauce evenly on top of each one.
  4. 4: Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and top with pepperoni slices.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and browned on the edges, about 10 minutes.
